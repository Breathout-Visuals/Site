About Me

I am Lucas Jacquot, a filmmaker driven by light, composition, and the pursuit of crafted, meaningful images. For the past two years, I’ve been shaping visual experiences across music videos, documentaries, and on-set grip work, always with a focus on atmosphere and precision.

I aim to create visuals that feel immersive and emotionally striking, bringing care, intention, and a cinematic eye to every project I collaborate on. Clients come to me for imagery that stands out and leaves a lasting impression.
