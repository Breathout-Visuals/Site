Après l’apparition personnifiée de cette solitude, Mayol se laisse peu à peu engloutir...
Description EN: 2024. Mainly shot at Mont-Bauron in Nice, ABYSSE was one of our first projects in the first year at Studio M. This 6-minute short film explores the gradual submersion of a man by his own solitude. After the personified appearance of this solitude, Mayol gradually lets himself be engulfed...
